Reservation Terms and Conditions

By submitting a reservation request via this form, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms and conditions governing the booking of your seating:

1. Submission and Recommendation

1.1. Advance Recommendation: While we endeavor to accommodate all requests, it is strongly recommended that you submit your reservation at least forty-eight (48) hours (two days) in advance of your desired dining time to ensure the best availability and preparation.

1.2. VIP Room Reservations: Please note that all reservations for the Exclusive VIP Chambers are strictly subject to availability and can only be finalized and confirmed via a direct telephone consultation with our staff.

2. Confirmation Protocol

2.1. Upon clicking "Confirm," your request enters our system for processing. You will receive an official confirmation message from our team within twenty-four (24) to forty-eight (48) hours of your submission.

2.2. A reservation is considered final and secured only upon receipt of this official confirmation message. If you do not receive a confirmation message within the stated timeframe, please contact us immediately.

3. Cancellation and Amendments

3.1. Once the reservation form is submitted, automatic cancellation is not permitted. The reservation is formally recognized as placed.

3.2. Should you need to amend or cancel your reservation after submission, you must contact us immediately via our designated telephone line. We will endeavor to assist you, but cancellation is not guaranteed.

4. Personal Data Usage

4.1. Privacy: The personal details provided in the reservation form (Name, Phone Number, Date, Time, Pax) will be used solely for the purpose of processing, confirming, and managing your booking, as well as communicating essential details regarding your dining experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy.
